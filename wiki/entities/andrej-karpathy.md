# Andrej Karpathy

AI researcher, educator, and entrepreneur. Co-founder of OpenAI, former Director of AI at Tesla, and creator of the [[llm-wiki-pattern]].

## Overview

Andrej Karpathy is one of the most influential figures in modern deep learning. He is known both for foundational research contributions and for his ability to explain complex AI concepts clearly, through blog posts, talks, and educational videos.

He proposed the [[llm-wiki-pattern]] — a methodology for maintaining a [[personal-knowledge-base]] using LLMs — observing that large language models are "very good at reading and writing" and can serve as automated librarians for personal knowledge management.

## Key Points

- Co-founder of OpenAI (2015)
- Director of AI at Tesla (2017–2022), where he led the Autopilot computer vision team
- Creator of the micrograd and nanoGPT educational projects
- Creator of the LLM Wiki pattern, described in a public GitHub Gist
- Advocate for clear, from-scratch implementations of neural networks as a learning tool
- Known for the "Software 2.0" essay arguing that neural networks are replacing traditionally written software
